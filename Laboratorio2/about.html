History

Welcome to Blender Studios, where every click tells a story, and every frame captures life's essence. Our journey began with a simple passion for freezing moments in time and turning them into timeless memories.

Founded by two friends, Briguitte Salas and Kenneth, Blender Studios was born out of a shared love for photography and a desire to share that love with the world. What started as a hobby soon blossomed into a full-fledged business, driven by our dedication to excellence and creativity.

At Blender Studios, we believe that photography is more than just capturing images; it's about capturing emotions, moments, and stories. Whether it's a wedding, a family portrait, or a corporate event, we approach each project with the same level of enthusiasm and attention to detail.

Our studio is more than just a space; it's a sanctuary for creativity and inspiration. Decked out with state-of-the-art equipment and cozy corners for brainstorming sessions, it's where magic happens.
