Costa Rica is one of the most biodiverse countries on the planet. Its territory is inhabited by more than 500,000 species between Flora and Fauna, which represents 4% of the global biodiversity. Costa Rica is a pioneer in ecotourism and is visited annually by thousands of tourists from around the world, attracted by its Volcanoes, Beaches, Rivers and of course for its Tropical Rainforest . Almost 25% of the Costa Rican territory is protected by SINAC (National System of Conservation Areas).
