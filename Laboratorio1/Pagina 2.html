About us

We are an Online Travel Agency duly registered with the Costa Rican Tourism Institute (I.C.T). We are not a tour operator, but we can guarantee that we will always do our best to help you coordinate your vacation while visiting Costa Rica at very affordable prices.

With us you can visit destinations like: The Rainforest, Arenal Volcano National Park in La Fortuna, Manuel Antonio National Park, Irazu Volcano, Visit Guanacaste beaches, Tortuga island, Poas Volcano or doing tours from Montezuma and Santa Teresa, among other destinations. Booking an activity with us..! We have agreements with various tour operators, hotels and different companies related to tourism industry in Costa Rica, so we can promote their services keeping the best prices on the market to our customers. When you book any tour through Tours Paradise, you can rest assured that we will be watching that everything is to your liking. We only work with Tour Operators that have all the required permits and insurance for tourism transportation, as well as accident insurance in Costa Rica.
